U.N. Sustanability in Turkey

The U.N. has created 17 sustanability goals to create a sustainable future aroud the globe. These goals are here to adress and take action to global issues by the year 2030.

The Importance

Goal #5

The goal that closely aligns with Women's Rights in Turkey is number 5. This goal is based on gender equality. The target 5.c says, "Adopt and strengthen sound policies and enforceable legislation for the promotion of gender equality and the empowerment of all women and girls at all levels." The importance of UN goal number 5 is to highlight and recognize the violence that occurs all around the world to women in particular. The harmful practices that have started to become normalized again need to be addressed and stopped. It's not the idea of women needing more but just for women to be seen as equals and not inferior.

Gender Equality

Goal 5 has strong ties to the women of Turkey because of all the issues that seem to be putting their rights backwards instead of forwards. They face gender-based violence, economic inequality, and limited political representation.

Their has been progress in ceratin areas like education but some traditions never die and thought it may be legal it doesn't mean the people of Turkey are happy aboit it. Women are having their freedom and lives threatened for just wanting to go to school or any other 'untraditional' women's value. Hopefully by commiting to goal number 5, Turkey aims to strengthen the protection of women and promote empowerment.
